It's Use In Weather Forecasting

Weather forecasts are made by collecting quantative numerical data about the current state of the atmosphere which is captured a variety of different sensors. In the case of weather models, data such as rain fall, temperature and wind speed are fed into the computer for it to analyse. This data is transmitted and collected centrally from thousdands of centers and put into a mathematical model in which predictions can be made from current conditions.

Parallel processing is generally used for complex calculations in mathematical weather models but this make its so that the equipment used is extremely expensive. Even though the more sophisticated and up-to-date the model means that the forecast predicted would be more accurate however it cannot account for freak weather patterns.
