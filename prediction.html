Current Uses

In today's world, computers are utilised for making ever-so important investment decisions. The computers often go through a complex decision tree (algorithm) to come to a conclusion on whether or not it would be a wise decision. The accuracy of the decision can be improved by adding more branches to the existing decision tree.

From being able to predict human behaviour to calculating the trajectory of dangerous comets close to the earth, analsysis and prediction have made a giant contribution to todays society. Right now as we speak, computers are handling large amounts of calculations and decisions in order to predict possible outbreak patterns of pathogens, tsunamis and even the climate.

What else can learn from predictive analystics? Well computers are now being taught how to predict the future and there has been some luck within the field. It might not be much however there has been a computer that has been able to predict a few seconds into the future. If this gets developed well then there is a plethora of progress that could happen within the society and the industry.
